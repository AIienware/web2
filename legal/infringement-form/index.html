It's possible that you might find something offensive even if that content doesn't violate Spotify's terms and conditions. If there's content on Spotify that you don't want to listen to, please consider the following options:

  • Use your own discretion and taste regarding what content you choose to listen to.
  • If the content relates to another Spotify user, "Unfollow" that user.
  • Speak out in any forums available to you about what you find offensive.

If you still wish to make us aware of certain content you find offensive:

  • Please provide the Spotify URI or HTTP link of the album/track(s) that you are referring to. Simply right click the track/album/artist that you're referring to in the Spotify client and select "Copy Spotify URI" or "Copy HTTP Link". If you do not have access to a Spotify URI/URL, please describe the content and its location as specifically as you can.

  • I state that the information in this notification is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that I am the owner of the exclusive right that is allegedly infringed, or an authorized agent for the owner.
  • I understand that my contact information will be passed to the alleged infringing party, and retained as long as required for legal purposes.
